JEX Group - Company Research
=============================

Jex Recruitment is a boutique executive search firm specializing in C-level and 
senior leadership placements, particularly in private equity-backed companies and 
investment holding groups across the Middle East.

CLIENT CONTEXT (Typical):
---------------------------
- UAE-based investment group or holding company
- Multiple business verticals/portfolio companies
- Need for centralized IT leadership
- Likely private equity or family office backed

APPROACH STRATEGY:
------------------
- Emphasize multi-entity IT governance experience
- Highlight budget management and ROI focus
- Demonstrate private equity/holding company understanding
- Show cultural fit for investment environment
