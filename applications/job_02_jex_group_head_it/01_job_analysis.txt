JEX - Group Head of Information Technology
=============================================

JOB ANALYSIS
============

Position Overview:
------------------
Position: Group Head of Information Technology
Source: Jex Recruitment
Location: Dubai, UAE
Level: Director/VP Level
Industry: Private Equity / Multi-Business Group

Key Responsibilities:
---------------------
1. Develop and execute group-wide IT strategy aligned with business objectives
2. Lead IT transformation initiatives across portfolio companies
3. Manage enterprise IT infrastructure and operations
4. Oversee cybersecurity and risk management frameworks
5. Drive digital innovation and emerging technology adoption
6. Build and lead high-performance IT organization
7. Manage technology budget and vendor relationships
8. Ensure business continuity and disaster recovery capabilities
9. Establish IT governance and compliance frameworks
10. Enable data-driven decision making across the group

Required Qualifications:
------------------------
- 15+ years IT experience with 8+ years in leadership roles
- Proven track record in multi-entity/group IT management
- Strong background in infrastructure, security, and digital transformation
- Experience in private equity or investment holding environment (preferred)
- Strategic mindset with hands-on technical capability
- Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills
- Financial acumen for ROI-based technology investments
- Regional experience (UAE/GCC) and cultural awareness

Company Context:
----------------
Jex Recruitment is a boutique executive search firm placing senior leaders in private equity backed companies. The client is a UAE-based investment group or holding company with multiple business verticals requiring centralized IT leadership.

Key Success Factors:
--------------------
1. Ability to balance standardization with business unit autonomy
2. Experience managing technology across diverse industries
3. Financial acumen for ROI-based technology investments
4. Change leadership for transformation programs
5. Regional experience (UAE/GCC) and cultural awareness
