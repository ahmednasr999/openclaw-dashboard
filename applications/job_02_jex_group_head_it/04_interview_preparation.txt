Group Head of IT - INTERVIEW PREPARATION
==========================================

LIKELY QUESTIONS:
=================

Q1: "How would you approach IT strategy for a group with diverse portfolio companies?"
A: Framework:
   - Assessment phase (current state, maturity levels)
   - Identify common platforms vs. specialized needs
   - Create IT governance model (centralized vs. federated)
   - Build 3-year roadmap with quick wins
   - Example: El Araby Group standardization approach

Q2: "Tell us about your experience managing IT budgets and demonstrating ROI."
A: Key Points:
   - Managed $12M budget at El Araby, $8M at TopMed
   - 25% cost reduction through cloud migration
   - Vendor consolidation saved 15% annually
   - Focus on business value, not just cost cutting

Q3: "How do you balance standardization with business unit autonomy?"
A: Approach:
   - Define non-negotiables (security, compliance, core platforms)
   - Allow flexibility for business-specific needs
   - Create governance committees with business representation
   - Example: ERP core with configurable modules

Q4: "Describe your approach to cybersecurity in a multi-entity environment."
A: Framework:
   - Centralized security operations center
   - Standardized policies with entity-specific risk assessments
   - Regular audits and penetration testing
   - Incident response plan with clear escalation
