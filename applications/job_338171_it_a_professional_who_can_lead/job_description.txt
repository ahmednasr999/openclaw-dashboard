We are looking for a dynamic and technically proficient Digital Transformation Senior Officer who brings together strategic transformation capabilities with hands-on experience in application development. This hybrid role is ideal for a professional who can lead digital change initiatives, while also supporting in designing, developing, and deploying web and ERP solutions to drive business modernization. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in driving technology-enabled transformation programs, coupled with strong programming skills, system integration experience, and a solid understanding of enterprise applications.
Key Responsibilities
Digital Transformation Strategy & Execution
Contribute to the development and execution of the organization’s digital transformation roadmap.
Identify opportunities to optimize business processes through automation, digitization, or technology enablement.
Evaluate digital platforms and AI solutions (e.g., chatbots, predictive analytics, NLP tools, generative AI) and support implementation in line with business priorities.
Work with leadership to ensure transformation initiatives align with strategic goals and operational priorities.
Application Development & Integration
Support the design, develop, test, and deploy web applications and ERP modules using modern frameworks and programming languages.
Assist in building or customizing features on platforms such as Microsoft Dynamics 365, Oracle, or similar ERP systems.
Collaborate with backend and frontend developers to deliver scalable, secure, and maintainable solutions.
Integrate third-party APIs, cloud services, and automation tools into enterprise systems
AI Use Case Discovery & Innovation Enablement
Collaborate with business units to identify high-impact AI use cases across operations, customer service, HR, marketing, and other areas.
Support AI proof-of-concept (PoC) design, testing, and scaling for automation, personalization, or forecasting initiatives.
Help define success metrics, training data needs, and ethical considerations for AI-enabled solutions.
Monitor industry trends and emerging technologies to inform AI adoption and innovation planning.
Project Management
Lead and execute transformation projects using Agile, Waterfall, or hybrid methodologies, ensuring on-time, within-budget delivery.
Manage cross-functional project teams spanning business, IT, operations, and vendors.
Define project scopes, objectives, milestones, and KPIs.
Business Analysis
Conduct in-depth analysis of current business processes and systems to identify inefficiencies or improvement areas.
Gather and document business and technical requirements through stakeholder workshops, interviews, and workflow reviews.
Translate business needs into functional specifications for IT and development teams.
Perform gap analysis, impact assessments, and feasibility studies.
Application Oversight & Digital Solutions
Support the planning, design, and implementation of new digital applications and platforms.
Oversee application lifecycle management, including user requirements, vendor coordination, testing, and rollout.
Ensure digital platforms meet business needs and compliance standards.
Collaborate with IT and technical teams to manage enhancements, feedback loops, and user adoption.
Stakeholder Engagement & Change Management
Serve as a liaison between business users, IT, and leadership to ensure alignment across digital projects.
Lead change management efforts to support successful technology adoption.
Develop training plans, user guides, and communication strategies to support transformation initiatives.
Communicate project status, risks, and outcomes to stakeholders and decision-makers.

Bachelor’s degree in business, Information systems, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.

Proven ability to lead medium-to-large-scale digital or IT transformation projects.
Hands-on experience with ERP platforms (Microsoft Dynamics, Oracle ERP, etc.).
Proficiency in project management and business analysis tools (e.g., Jira, MS Project, Visio)

Change Management (e.g., Prosci, ADKAR)
Exposure to enterprise platforms such as Microsoft Dynamics CRM and Oracle ERP.
Functional Competencies
Digital transformation planning, process optimization, and automation
