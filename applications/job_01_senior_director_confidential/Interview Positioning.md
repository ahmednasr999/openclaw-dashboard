## Key Stories to Prepare

### Story 1: Driving Transformation at Scale
**Context:** TopMed multi-country rollout
**Challenge:** Fragmented operations across 6 countries, low technology adoption
**Actions:** 
- Built governance framework tying AI adoption to KPIs
- Created mentorship program for regional leaders
- Established performance dashboards visible to C-suite
**Results:** 95% AI adoption, 233x performance improvement, 3x profit increase
**Lesson:** Transformation succeeds when governance meets human development

### Story 2: Building High-Performance Teams
**Context:** Talabat hyper-growth phase
**Challenge:** Scale engineering team 10x while maintaining velocity
**Actions:**
- Restructured teams around microservices architecture
- Implemented structured mentoring program
- Created clear career ladders with succession planning
**Results:** Scaled from 30K to 7M daily orders, 60% reduction in onboarding time
**Lesson:** Operational scalability requires intentional talent architecture

### Story 3: Crisis Turnaround
**Context:** PaySky implementation challenges
**Challenge:** Failing ERP project, budget overruns, stakeholder confidence low
**Actions:**
- Implemented rigorous governance and checkpoint process
- Restructured project around business outcomes not features
- Established executive steering committee with weekly KPIs
**Results:** Project delivered, $12.4B transaction platform operational
**Lesson:** Operational governance restores confidence and delivers results

## Questions to Ask Them

### Strategic
- "What's the most significant operational challenge the organization is facing in the next 12 months?"
- "How does the board measure transformation success?"
- "What's the history of transformation initiatives here—what worked and what didn't?"

### Tactical
- "What does success look like in this role at 6, 12, and 24 months?"
- "What resources (budget, headcount, technology) are allocated to this transformation?"
- "Who are the key stakeholders I'll need to align with?"

### Cultural
- "How does the organization typically handle resistance to change?"
- "What's the leadership culture—collaborative or directive?"
- "How are decisions made at the executive level?"
