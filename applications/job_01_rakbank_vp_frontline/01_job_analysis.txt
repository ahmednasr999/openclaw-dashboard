RAKBANK - VP Frontline Experience, Onboarding Transformation & Alternate Channels
==================================================================================

JOB ANALYSIS
============

Position Overview:
------------------
Reporting to: EVP & Head of Retail Customer Acquisition
Location: Dubai, UAE
Level: Vice President (Mid-Senior)
Industry: Banking

Key Responsibilities:
---------------------
1. Own end-to-end customer onboarding journey across all retail channels (branches, elite banking, direct sales, digital, field sales, Employee Banking)
2. Identify and eliminate customer pain points across physical and digital onboarding journeys
3. Ensure consistent and seamless onboarding experience across all retail products and customer segments
4. Design and implement structured onboarding frameworks for frontline sales teams
5. Partner with HR, L&D, and business leaders to align onboarding and training
6. Track onboarding effectiveness and drive continuous improvement using data
7. Lead strategy and execution of alternate acquisition channels (kiosks, LAM, digital partnerships)
8. Design tailored onboarding journeys for each channel and partner type
9. Monitor channel and partner performance
10. Drive enhancements to digital onboarding journeys across mobile and assisted channels
11. Harmonize onboarding processes across branches, kiosks, and field sales
12. Work closely with Product, Technology, Operations, Risk, and Compliance teams

Required Skills:
----------------
- Strong expertise in retail banking onboarding journeys, including KYC/AML, CDD, and digital identity solutions
- Deep understanding of digital and alternate acquisition channels within banking
- Proven experience in customer experience design, journey mapping, and analytics-driven optimization
- Strong programme management, governance, and change-management capabilities
- Data-driven mindset with ability to translate insights into measurable outcomes
- Strategic thinker with ability to translate vision into execution
- Strong stakeholder management and influencing skills across business, operations, technology, risk, and compliance
- Customer-focused with sound commercial judgement
- Resilient and adaptable with hands-on approach to problem-solving
- Proven ability to build, mentor, and lead high-performing teams

Company Context:
----------------
RAKBANK (National Bank of Ras Al Khaimah) is one of the UAE's leading banks with strong focus on retail banking and SME lending. Known for innovation in digital banking and customer experience.

Key Stakeholders to Engage:
---------------------------
- EVP & Head of Retail Customer Acquisition (direct report)
- HR & Learning & Development teams
- Product Management teams
- Technology/IT teams
- Operations teams
- Risk & Compliance teams
- Branch network and field sales leadership
