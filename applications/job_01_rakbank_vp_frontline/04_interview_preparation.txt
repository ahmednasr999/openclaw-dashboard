RAKBANK VP Frontline Experience - INTERVIEW PREPARATION
========================================================

LIKELY INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & ANSWER FRAMEWORKS:
===============================================

1. "Tell us about your experience with customer onboarding transformation."
   ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Framework: STAR Method
   - Situation: PaySky's fragmented onboarding causing 40% drop-off
   - Task: Transform end-to-end onboarding across all channels
   - Action: Implemented digital KYC, unified process, trained teams
   - Result: 50% improvement in NPS, reduced TAT from 5 days to 24 hours

2. "How do you approach eliminating customer pain points?"
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   Framework: Process + Example
   - Start with customer journey mapping and data analysis
   - Identify friction points through analytics and feedback
   - Prioritize based on impact vs. effort
   - Implement quick wins while building long-term solutions
   - Example: At El Araby, reduced cart abandonment by 25%

3. "Describe your experience with KYC/AML in a banking context."
   -------------------------------------------------------------
   Framework: Knowledge + Application
   - Understanding of regulatory requirements (CBUAE guidelines)
   - Implementation of eKYC and digital identity solutions
   - Balance between compliance and customer experience
   - Experience at PaySky with merchant onboarding compliance

4. "How do you manage stakeholders across Risk, Compliance, and Technology?"
   --------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Framework: Collaboration Approach
   - Establish shared goals and metrics upfront
   - Regular cross-functional workshops
   - Data-driven discussions (not opinions)
   - Escalation paths and governance structure
   - Example: Successfully launched product requiring alignment of 5 departments

5. "What's your approach to channel performance optimization?"
   ------------------------------------------------------------
   Framework: Data-Driven Methodology
   - Define clear KPIs for each channel
   - Implement real-time dashboards
   - A/B testing for journey improvements
   - Regular performance reviews and optimization cycles
   - Resource allocation based on ROI
