RAKBANK (National Bank of Ras Al Khaimah) - Company Research
=============================================================

COMPANY OVERVIEW
================

Basic Information:
------------------
- Full Name: National Bank of Ras Al Khaimah (RAKBANK)
- Founded: 1976
- Headquarters: Ras Al Khaimah, UAE
- Type: Public Joint Stock Company
- Listed on: Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange (ADX)
- Market Cap: ~AED 10+ billion
- Employees: ~3,000+

Business Segments:
------------------
1. Retail Banking (Core business)
   - Personal banking accounts and deposits
   - Personal loans and mortgages
   - Credit cards
   - SME banking

2. Business Banking
   - SME lending and services
   - Corporate banking
   - Trade finance

3. Treasury & Investments

Strategic Focus Areas:
----------------------
- Digital banking innovation (RAKBANK Digital)
- Customer experience excellence
- SME banking leadership
- Islamic banking (via RAKislamic)
- Alternative channels and fintech partnerships

2. Right Size for Impact
