Al Ghurair - Senior Manager - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
=========================================================================

JOB ANALYSIS
============

Position Overview:
------------------
Position: Senior Manager - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
Company: Al Ghurair
Location: Dubai, UAE
Industry: Conglomerate (Holding Company)
Level: Mid-Senior

Key Responsibilities:
---------------------
1. Develop and implement AI/ML strategy across Al Ghurair group companies
2. Architect scalable machine learning solutions for business problems
3. Lead AI Center of Excellence and build data science capabilities
4. Drive data-driven decision making across the organization
5. Implement MLOps practices and model governance
6. Collaborate with business units to identify AI opportunities
7. Manage relationships with technology vendors and partners
8. Ensure ethical AI practices and compliance
9. Build and mentor data science teams
10. Deliver production-grade AI solutions with measurable business impact

Required Qualifications:
------------------------
- 10+ years technology experience with 5+ years in AI/ML
- Proven track record delivering AI solutions to production
- Strong software engineering and architecture background
- Experience with cloud ML platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Business acumen to translate problems into AI solutions
- Leadership skills to build and mentor data science teams
- Expertise in deep learning, NLP, computer vision, or predictive analytics

Company Context:
----------------
Al Ghurair is one of the largest family-owned conglomerates in the UAE with diversified interests:
- Manufacturing and Industry
- Real Estate and Construction
- Investments and Financial Services
- Education
- Automotive

The group is investing in digital transformation and sees AI as a competitive differentiator across its portfolio.

Key Success Factors:
--------------------
1. Ability to identify high-value AI use cases across diverse industries
2. Technical depth combined with business translation skills
3. Experience productionizing ML models at scale
4. Change management for AI adoption
5. Cross-functional collaboration with business units
