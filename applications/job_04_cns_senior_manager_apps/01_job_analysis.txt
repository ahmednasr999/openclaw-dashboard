CNS Middle East - Senior Manager - Applications

JOB ANALYSIS
============

------------------

Location: Dubai, UAE
Industry: IT Services / Software Development
Level: Mid-Senior

2. Manage client relationships and project delivery
3. Oversee software development lifecycle (SDLC)
4. Ensure quality standards and SLA compliance

- Team leadership and people management skills
- Knowledge of SDLC methodologies (Agile, Waterfall)
- UAE/GCC regional experience preferred

- Digital transformation and application modernization

Key Success Factors:

1. Client satisfaction and relationship management
