# Application Strategy: Bisher & Partners - Senior Advisory Partner

## Position Analysis
- **Role:** Senior Advisory Partner
- **Level:** Partner/Executive level
- **Location:** UAE/GCC
- **Priority:** HIGH

---

## Application Approach: **ENGAGE FIRST, THEN APPLY**

### Rationale:
1. **Partner-Level Role:** Senior advisory positions often prioritize relationship and cultural fit
2. **Boutique Firm:** Smaller firms value personal connections and network introductions
3. **Due Diligence:** Important to understand firm's client base, culture, and partnership structure before applying
4. **Network Leverage:** GCC advisory community is tight-knit; warm introductions highly effective

---

## Timeline

### Week 1: Network Engagement
- [ ] Identify current partners and leadership at Bisher & Partners via LinkedIn
- [ ] Research firm's recent deals, clients, and market positioning
- [ ] Find mutual connections for warm introductions
- [ ] Engage with firm's content on LinkedIn (posts, articles)

### Week 2: Strategic Application
- [ ] Tailor CV emphasizing advisory and partnership experience
- [ ] Submit application with personalized cover letter
- [ ] Follow up with direct outreach to hiring partner
- [ ] Continue networking activities

### Week 3-4: Interview Preparation
- [ ] Prepare case studies on advisory engagements
- [ ] Research firm's key clients and industries
- [ ] Develop point of view on GCC advisory market trends
- [ ] Prepare for partner-level interviews

4. **Alumni Networks:** Intel, Microsoft, PMI network in UAE

## Interview Preparation

### Key Stories to Prepare
1. **Advisory Success:** C-suite advisory delivering AED 50M+ value
2. **Practice Building:** Establishing PMO as internal consulting function
3. **Client Development:** Building lasting executive relationships
4. **Deal Experience:** Technology due diligence and vendor negotiations

### Questions to Ask
- What is the firm's strategic vision for the next 3-5 years?
- How does the partnership structure and governance work?
- What industries and clients is the firm prioritizing?
- What does success look like for a new partner in year one?
