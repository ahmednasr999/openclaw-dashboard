CNS (Computer Network Systems) is a technology company in the middle-east & has over 30 years of successful achievements and long-term partnerships with distinct customers across the region.

We are an IT distributor & our expertise are in Banking & Financial Service Insurance, Technology Enablement, IT Outsourcing, Cyber security, Digital Infrastructure, Hybrid Cloud, Digital Workplace, Customer Experience, Digital Transformation, and Block Chain.

We are a Part of the Ghobash Group of Companies, CNS spread across the region with offices in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Oman, and Kuwait providing a holistic approach that covers all aspects of information communication technology system integration, consultancy, operation, managed services, and IT outsourcing.

CNS also has state-of-the-art network operation centers in Abu Dhabi and Dubai that develop in-house software and applications providing end-to-end solutions to our customers and partners.

Job Description

We are seeking a highly experienced and strategic Senior Manager – Applications to lead our Applications Business Unit (BU). This role will be responsible for owning the business unit’s strategy, go-to-market (GTM) execution, growth, and profitability. The ideal candidate will bring strong leadership, technical depth, and a clear understanding of enterprise business processes to support CNS’s overall objectives and deliver client-centric solutions.

Duties & Responsibilities



Strategic & Business Leadership
Develop and execute strategic plans aligned with CNS’s business goals for the Applications BU.
Define go-to-market strategies to drive revenue growth and market presence.
Identify emerging technologies, market trends, and business opportunities.
Ensure all initiatives are in line with business targets and timelines.
Delivery & Operational Excellence
Oversee the full software development lifecycle – from requirement gathering and planning to deployment and support.
Ensure delivery excellence, including timeliness, quality standards, and budget adherence.
Establish and enforce best practices, development methodologies, and performance standards.
Client Engagement & Satisfaction
Engage with key clients to understand business needs and technical requirements.
Build and maintain long-term relationships to enhance satisfaction and retention.
Address escalations and ensure proactive communication with clients.
Financial Management
Own and manage the BU’s P&L including revenue forecasting, budgeting, and cost control.
Implement cost-optimization strategies while pursuing new revenue opportunities.
People & Cross-Functional Collaboration
Lead, mentor, and develop high-performing technical and functional teams.
Collaborate closely with sales, marketing, pre-sales, and other BUs to ensure alignment on project and delivery goals.
Promote a culture of innovation, collaboration, and accountability.
Risk, Compliance & Governance
Ensure projects and solutions are compliant with industry regulations, data protection standards, and internal policies.
Proactively identify and mitigate project and business risks.

Education:

Proven experience in software development, with at least 8+ years in a leadership role overseeing application development, delivery, or business unit management.


Skills & Abilities:

Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills.
Strong understanding of business processes, market dynamics, and evolving customer needs.
Demonstrated ability to develop and execute strategic plans, manage budgets effectively, and drive results through cross-functional collaboration and performance management.
