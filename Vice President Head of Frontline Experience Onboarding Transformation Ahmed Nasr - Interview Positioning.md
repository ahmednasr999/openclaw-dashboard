## Questions They'll Ask & How to Answer

### Q: "You don't have direct retail banking experience. Why should we hire you?"
**Your Answer:** 
"Customer onboarding challenges are universal—whether it's a patient or a banking customer. The friction points are the same: too many steps, unclear requirements, handoffs between departments. I've solved these problems at scale in a highly regulated healthcare environment, which is arguably more complex than banking. I bring fresh perspective combined with deep onboarding expertise that will accelerate your transformation."

### Q: "How would you approach our frontline experience challenges?"
**Your Answer:**
"I'd start with the frontline themselves. At TopMed, I discovered our staff struggled with the same pain points as customers. I designed structured onboarding frameworks that made their jobs easier, not harder. Then I used data—tracking where customers dropped off, which steps caused confusion—and eliminated those friction points. The result: happier employees and 95% digital adoption."

### Q: "Tell us about scaling alternate channels."
**Your Answer:**
"At PaySky, I led strategy for digital partnerships and field marketing—essentially your kiosk and LAM equivalents. The key is tailoring the onboarding journey for each channel. A customer acquired through a digital partner has different expectations than one walking into a branch. I designed channel-specific journeys that maintained our brand standards while optimizing for each context."

### Q: "How do you handle compliance in onboarding?"
**Your Answer:**
"Compliance isn't the enemy of good experience—poor process design is. At EMP, I managed banking projects where KYC was mandatory, but we found ways to make it seamless. We front-loaded data collection, used progressive profiling, and integrated identity verification tools. The result? Full compliance with faster onboarding, not despite it."
