## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings/Bold:** Telegraf (sans-serif)
- **Body/Light Text:** Libre Franklin Light (sans-serif)

### Font Weights Used
- Light (300) - Body text, subtitles
- Regular (400) - General text  
- Bold (600-700) - Headings, emphasis

---

## Visual Style Characteristics

### Design Principles
- **Minimalist:** Clean white backgrounds with generous whitespace
- **High Contrast:** Black text on white for maximum readability
- **Professional:** Corporate consulting aesthetic
- **Modern:** Sans-serif typography, clean lines

### Common Elements
- Thin black divider lines (`1px solid #000000`)
- Left accent bars (`6px` width, `#304254` color)
- Subtle corner accents (low opacity borders)
- Professional photography integration
